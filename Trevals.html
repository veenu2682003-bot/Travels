Traveler's Tips

1. Always carry your ID and travel documents safely.
2. Pack light and keep only necessary items.
3. Keep some local currency with you.
4. Follow local rules and traditions of the place you visit.
5. Stay hydrated and eat clean food.
6. Keep emergency contact numbers saved on your phone.
7. Always inform someone about your travel plans.
8. Do not share your personal information with strangers.
